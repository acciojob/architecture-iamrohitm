Objective

Discuss factors that are important in the design of bedrooms. Plan the size and location of closets for a typical residence and develop furniture arrangements for key rooms.

Plan bathrooms that follow solid design principles. Design the service area of a home by applying good circulation and storage concepts, including an efficient laundry and garage.

Design a functional kitchen that meets a family's needs, selecting appliances and work-triangle relationships appropriate for the plan. Apply design principles to living and dining rooms, integrating furniture with door and window placement.

Finally, design a clear, functional entry and foyer that introduces the architectural character of the home.
